Enable Desktop Notifications

Mooltipass requires permission to display Chrome notifications for interactions between you and the Mooltipass device. Please follow the instructions to enable notifications.

  1. Click the bell icon in the lower-right corner of your screen on Windows, or in the top menu bar on your Mac
  2. Click the gear icon
  3. Check the "Mooltipass Extension" box

After activating the extension, the red bar on top changes to a green confirmation bar to indicate that everything is correctly configured.
