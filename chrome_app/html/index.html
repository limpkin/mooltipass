Developers Interactions

This page provides special interactions with the Mooltipass device, which should usually not be required.

Factory Reset a Card

To reuse a paired card on another Mooltipass device, you need to reset the card. Once done, the card is no longer paired with the previous device. This interaction is only available for unknown cards.

Import Media Bundle

Import a new media bundle on the Mooltipass device.

 

Jump To Bootloader

Send a packet to instruct the Mooltipass device to jump to the bootloader. This will break your device!

Synchronization

You can backup the credentials stored inside your device to a file or cloud storage, and restore them later in case your Mooltipass device gets lost or broken. Your passwords will always be encrypted with your personal security key, which is only stored inside your key cards. Your credentials are safe, even if someone gets access to your backup files.

File Storage

Store your credentials in a file on your local computer.

Cloud Storage

Store your credentials in your Google account. Your backup can be accessed from all Chrome browsers where you are logged in.

Integrity Check

Scan your device for errors and correct them automatically.
